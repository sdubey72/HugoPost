Bar graph and Histogram

In this post we’ll learn to draw Bar-graph and Histogram using the following libraries. > dplyr , > Lahman , > ggplot2 and > ggiraph

Here we will all the teams who played in the year 1980’s.

data1<-Teams%>%
  filter(yearID==1980)%>%
  select(teamID,HR)

and plot a bar-graph !

ggplot()+
  geom_bar(data = data1 , aes(x=teamID,y=HR),stat = "identity",color="red",fill="blue")

Here it is ! But it looks very cluttered . Lets see if we can change the axis and make it more readable.

data2<-Teams%>%
  filter(yearID==1980)%>%
  select(name,HR)%>%
  arrange(HR)

data2$name<-factor(data2$name,levels = data2$name)

Now lets try to do some histogram.

df<-Master%>%
  filter(!is.na(weight))%>%
  select(weight)
ggplot()+
  geom_histogram(data=df,aes(x=weight),color="white",fill="blue")+
  ggtitle("baseball players weights")
